Who We Are

A Reliable Engineering & MEP Partner

NorthFort Engineering Limited delivers cost-effective, high-quality, and safe engineering, procurement, construction, and facility solutions across Africa.

  • Safety First: Zero compromises on QHSE standards.
  • Reliable Delivery: Complete projects on budget and on schedule.
  • Local Expertise: Maximum utilization of skilled local manpower.

Our Mission

NorthFort Engineering Limited strives to be the global leader in the provision of quality, cost effective and promptly delivered services to the oil and gas and allied industries worldwide. Best safety practice is our number one priority in the delivery of services to meet or surpass our client expectations.

Our Vision

To be the best in the oil and gas and allied services delivery, through maximum utilization of local manpower resources, international standards compliance, and advanced technical innovation to meet the challenging demand of clients' quality service delivery.
