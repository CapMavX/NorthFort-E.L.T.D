Energy Sector Support

Technical Oil & Gas Infrastructure Services

NorthFort Engineering Limited is highly active in technical and procurement support for the oil and gas sector. We provide upstream, midstream, and downstream services, aligning our HSE plans with strict industry guidelines.

With a leadership team deeply rooted in Nigerian oil and gas operations (such as GM Haruna Akpata and Quality Manager Jude Owunma), we deliver quality engineering design, procurement contracts, and industrial station maintenance.

Our Oil & Gas Scope of Work

Front-End Engineering Design (FEED)

Conceptual studies, FEED, and detailed mechanical and process engineering of oil & gas piping networks, flow stations, manifolds, and distribution pipelines.

Industrial Procurement & Valves

Procurement and supply of premium fittings, tubulars, and specialized industrial valves (ball, butterfly, relief, check, gate, air, and primer valves) conforming to API codes.

Gas Station HVAC Services

Specialized maintenance of complex industrial cooling, ventilation, air filtration, and HVAC systems at gas compressor stations in Lagos, Ogun, Cotonou, Lome, and Accra.

Instrumentation & Controls

Supply of advanced metering systems, control panel instrumentation, process monitors, and measurement/regulation units to ensure piping system pressure balance.

Fluids, Chemicals & Additives

Supply of specialized drilling mud fluids, chemical processing agents, inhibitors, and fluid additives for drilling campaigns and production operations.

Paint & Corrosion Prevention

Procurement of corrosion control paints, sandblasting equipment, cathodic protection monitors, and protective coatings for marine and onshore piping structures.
