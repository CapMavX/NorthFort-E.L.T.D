What We Excel At

Integrated Engineering Solutions

At NorthFort, we deliver technical services and procurement solutions across three critical sectors. Each service is executed to comply with international codes, client specifications, and absolute safety guidelines.

Sector 01

Procurement & Supply Services

We procure and supply high-quality industrial components, pipelines, instrumentation systems, safety equipment, and specialty chemicals to the oil and gas and construction sectors.

  • Valves (Ball, Butterfly, Gate, Relief, Check, Control)
  • Instrumentation Panels, Compressors & Metering Systems
  • Generators, Mud Fluids & Paint Corrosion Inhibitors
