Supply Chain Excellence

Industrial Procurement & Component Supply

NorthFort Engineering Limited delivers premium, reliable procurement and supply chain solutions to support large-scale construction, MEP installations, and oil & gas operations across Africa.

We leverage strong partnerships with international manufacturers to distribute certified valves, fittings, tubulars, and instrumentation panels that comply with ISO standards and client technical specifications.

Valves & Fittings

Supply of ball, butterfly, relief, control, check, gate, air, and primer valves. Also sourcing premium fittings and industrial tubulars.

Instrumentation & Controls

Sourcing instrumentation panels, compressors, metering systems, and measurement, regulation, and control equipment.

Power & Generators

Procurement of power generators, power distribution equipment, cables, and transformers for commercial and site operations.

Drilling Fluids & Mud

Supply of specialty chemicals, drilling mud fluids, and additives for exploration and oilfield drilling operations.

Environmental Remediation

Environmental remediation and control equipment designed to mitigate ecological impacts and satisfy environmental compliance rules.

Corrosion Prevention

Sourcing industrial paints, corrosion prevention coatings, and surface treatment equipment to protect pipelines and structures.
