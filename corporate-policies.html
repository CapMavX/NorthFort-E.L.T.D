Safety First

Health, Safety & Environment (HSE)

NorthFort Engineering Limited has established laid down plans to conduct all aspects of her activities in such a way as to guarantee, enhance and promote the safety of personnel, equipments or properties that may be directly affected by such operations at all times anywhere a project is undertaken.

Efforts shall be made to protect and conserve the environment where such activities are carried out. Our company will at no time compromise, neglect, or overlook safety ethics, standards and practices no matter the level of urgency for the project in consideration. All ongoing activities shall be suspended as soon as it is recognized that a safety guarantee can no longer be maintained.

Core HSE Policies

These policies serve as HSE policy management guides on all company operations:

  • Environmental Policy
  • Security & Journey Management
  • Waste Management Policy
  • No Smoking Policy
  • Personnel Protective Equipment (PPE)
  • Health & Safety Statements

QMS Conformance

Our Quality Management System (QMS) conforms to the requirements of the ISO 9001:2008 management system. The scope of our quality controls covers the provision of:

  • Quality Manuals & Work Procedures
  • Project-specific Work Instructions
  • Standard Quality Record Tracking
  • Internal Audits & Management Reviews
Operational Quality

Quality Management Plan

It is the plan of NorthFort Engineering Limited to conduct her activities in such a way as to meet international codes, local and corporate codes, standards, client specifications and quality requirements with the objective of achieving absolute Customer Satisfaction.

Implementation of internal audits and periodic management reviews enables the sustenance of NorthFort corporate Quality management plans and objectives.

Indigenous Participation

Nigerian Content Development Plan

In line with the corporate sustainability policy, NorthFort Engineering Limited has established a local content policy based on sustainability and local capacity building principles.

Core Local Content Principles

  • Transparent sustainability communications within the company.
  • Providing training to develop human capital and build competencies.
  • Protection of environment, social rights, and contribution to local economics.
  • Reliable verification system for supplier sustainability.

Short & Long Term Implementation

  • Development and improvement of construction facilities.
  • Strong involvement in state of the art technology.
  • Advocating for indigenous participation by Nigerian companies.
  • Attending local content seminars and development workshops.
